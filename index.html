DIEREVA

A musical project by Gigi Giustiniani

Mycelial Electronic Downtempo  ·  Milan, Italy  ·  Active since 2021

Electronic musician and documentary filmmaker. Diereva weaves analogue synthesis, field recordings and live improvisation into cinematic, introspective soundscapes. Releases on independent labels across Russia, Mexico, Turkey, Ukraine, Portugal, Germany and the UK.

Artist Statement

Artistic Practice

Diereva is rooted in the idea of music as a living ecosystem — layered, interconnected, growing. The name itself evokes a tree: the project draws on organic field recordings, analogue synthesis and the unpredictable energy of live improvisation. Every track is built from physical materials — sounds collected from real environments, processed through analogue hardware and shaped in real time during live performance with Ableton, APC40 and synthesizers.

The music sits in the space between downtempo, ambient and organic electronica — unhurried, textured and cinematic. Tracks range from intimate studio compositions to extended live explorations of 20–30 minutes. This dual mode — studio precision and live improvisation — is central to Diereva's identity.

As a documentary filmmaker, Gigi specialises in social documentaries and mountain films, with fieldwork in Mauritania, Benin, Brazil and Ethiopia. This practice of deep listening and image-making in remote human environments is inseparable from Diereva's organic, place-rooted approach to sound.
