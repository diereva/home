DIEREVA

A musical project by Gigi Giustiniani

Mycelial Electronic Downtempo · Milan, Italy · Active since 2021

Electronic musician and documentary filmmaker. Diereva weaves analogue synthesis, field recordings and live improvisation into cinematic, introspective soundscapes. Releases on independent labels across Russia, Mexico, Turkey, Ukraine, Portugal, Germany and the UK.
